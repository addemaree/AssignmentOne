What We Do

Tri-Mar is family owned since 1992.

We do not expect your business. Rather we want to earn your business through our commitment to consistent quality and reliable service. This is paramount to us, and we work every day to make sure you are more than satisfied.

We pride ourselves on our research into the newest trends of color, and spend many hours and resources to bring these to market.

We partner with a limited number of factories to bring the highest and most consistent quality for use with your creative designs. We also partner with distributors across the United States and Canada to make sure you receive reliable hands on attention. When you choose Tri-Mar as your source for your ribbon accessories, know you have a committed design partner standing with you!
